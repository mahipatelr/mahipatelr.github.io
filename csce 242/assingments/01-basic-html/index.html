Breakfast

Breakfast is known to be the most important meal of the day. The meal provides energy and nutrients for the body as people report feeling more focused and alert throughout the day. Building a routine with starting off each morning with a balanced breakfast can lead to increased productivity. Whereas, skipping breakfast can lead to fatigue and lack of motivation.

Some popular breakfast foods include, but are not limited to: eggs, yogurt, fruit, oatmeal, toast, pancakes, waffles, and smoothies. Eating a combination of these foods makes a more nutritious breakfast. A balanced breakfast of various food groups supports a healthy lifestyle. The food groups include, fruits, vegetables, grains, protein, and dairy.

Popular Breakfast Combinations

  • Eggs and toast
  • Yogurt with fruit and granola
  • Cereal and milk
