Getting Started with TrustPay

A quick guide to using your simulated wallet and getting familiar with the demo environment.

Introduction

Welcome to TrustPay's demo wallet! This guide walks you through the essentials so you can start exploring simulated transactions, tracking balances, and practicing budgeting — all without real money.

Step 1 — Explore the Wallet

Open the Wallet page to view your simulated balance and the activity chart. Use the "Simulated Balance" pill to quickly recognize demo mode and practice interpreting transactions.

Step 2 — Browse Transactions

Visit Transactions to see a list of sample purchases, income, and transfers. You can use these to learn how categories and balances update over time.

Step 3 — Try Lessons

Head to the Learn page for guided lessons like Getting Started and Budgeting Basics — complete checkpoints and track your progress in the Progress Tracker.

Note: This demo environment uses sample data only — no real money is involved.
